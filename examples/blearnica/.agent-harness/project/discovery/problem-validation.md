# Problem Validation

## Assumed Problem

Learners do not know which foundation topics they are missing, so they waste
time studying randomly or give up when new topics feel too difficult.

## Audience To Validate

- Middle school students.
- High school students.
- First-year university students.
- Self-learners studying math, English, or programming.

## Interview Questions

1. When learning a new topic, what usually blocks you?
2. Do you know exactly which earlier topics you are weak at?
3. What tools or people help you today?
4. What is inconvenient about your current approach?
5. Have you paid money or spent meaningful time to solve this?

## Evidence The Problem Is Real

- Learners already use tutors, videos, worksheets, and apps.
- Learners can describe specific topics where they feel lost.
- Parents and students pay for repeated support.

## Decision

Continue.

Rationale: the problem is repeated, painful, and already has paid workarounds.
