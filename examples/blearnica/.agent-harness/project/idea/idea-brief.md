# Idea Brief

## Idea

Blearnica is a personalized learning platform that helps learners identify weak
knowledge areas, follow a clear learning path, practice step by step, and track
progress over time.

## Problem

Learners often study in fragments, do not know exactly where their foundation
is weak, and lack continuous guidance.

## Primary Users

Students, self-learners, and early university learners who need structured
support.

## Desired Outcome

Learners know their current level, understand weak topics, follow a suitable
path, and see measurable progress.

## Why Now

AI-assisted diagnosis, content generation, and adaptive practice make
personalized learning more accessible than before.
