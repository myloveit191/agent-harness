## Interview Questions

Ask about current behavior, not whether users like the proposed product.

1. How do you solve this today?
2. What is difficult or frustrating about that?
3. How often does this happen?
4. What tools, services, or workarounds do you use?
5. Have you paid money or spent meaningful time on this?

## Evidence The Problem Is Real

- Users already search for a solution.
- Users spend time or money on the problem.
- Users describe specific pain without prompting.
- The same pain repeats across multiple users.
